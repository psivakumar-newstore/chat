NewStore has partnered with
Fiskaltrust
to achieve compliance in Austria. All fiscal transactions are processed through the integration with Fiskaltrust. Fiskaltrust uses hardware/software signature creation units for signing transactions.
Fiscal activations
In stores operating in Austria, a store must be fiscally activated before orders can be created via NewStore Associate app.
Fiscal cashbox activation
The fiscal cashbox activation process in Austria includes the following steps:
Fiscal cashbox activation in the fiskaltrust Portal
Fiscal cashbox activation in the NewStore Omnichannel Manager
Fiscal cashbox activation in the fiskaltrust Portal
Important
FinanzOnline credentials must be set in the fiskaltrust PosOperator account before the fiscal activation of a store. For more information, see
FinanzOnline Management in fiskaltrust documentation
.
For stores in production environment, cashbox activation can be performed through
fiskaltrust Portal
For stores in non-production environment, cashbox activation can be performed through
fiskaltrust Sandbox Portal
Note
If you have a fiskaltrust PosDealer account, you can perform fiscal activation through the
Rollout Management
by using your PosDealer account.
If you don't have a fiskaltrust PosDealer account, contact the
support team
to walk you through the fiscal activation process.
Fiscal cashbox activation in the NewStore Omnichannel Manager
Important
A store must be already created within the NewStore platform before it can be fiscally activated. For more details, see
Creating a store
.
To fiscally activate a store, a fiscal cashbox can be activated within the NewStore platform via Omnichannel Manager. For more details, see
Activating fiscal cashboxes
.
Following information is required to fiscally activate a store in Austria:
Fiskaltrust cashbox information
Fiskaltrust Cashbox ID - The cashbox ID of the Fiskaltrust cashbox.
Fiskaltrust Access Token - The access token of the Fiskaltrust cashbox.
After the fiscal activation, an automated notification will be sent to FinanzOnline by fiskaltrust.
Fiscal cashbox deactivation
Important
Fiscal cashbox deactivation step is only required for cashboxes of the production tenants. For non-production tenants, the fiscal cashbox deactivation step can be skipped.
When a store is no longer operational within the NewStore platform:
The fiscal cashbox within the NewStore platform must be deactivated.
The fiscal cashbox within the
fiskaltrust portal
must be deactivated and a
closing receipt
must be generated.
The closing receipt is automatically generated by the NewStore platform upon fiscal cashbox deactivation.
After the cashbox deactivation, an automated notification will be sent to FinanzOnline by fiskaltrust.
Important
Your content goes hereFinanzOnline notification is only sent when fiskaltrust Carefree package is used, and the FinanzOnline credentials are set.
For more information, see
fiskaltrust documentation
.
To request the deactivation of a fiscal cashbox within the NewStore platform, contact the
support team
.
Receipts for stores
Based on fiscal regulations that apply to stores operating in Austria, these additional features must be included in the receipts:
All receipts must display the status of the fiscal signature and the QR code of the fiscal signature.
To modify the fiscal signature template for Austria, See
Customizing a template
.
Important
NewStore platform provides fiscal transaction information that is required to be printed in fiscal receipts. It is responsibility of the retailer to ensure compliance of the receipt templates with the local fiscal regulations.
Fiscal reports
For stores operating in Austria, required fiscal reports in the given formats are provided by Fiskaltrust and can be accessed in the
fiskaltrust Portal
.
POS-Archive DEP 7
E131
An
annual receipt
will be automatically created before the first transaction of the next year. After the
annual receipt
is created, an automated validation will be performed by fiskaltrust.
Important
FinanzOnline notification is only sent when fiskaltrust Carefree package is used, and the FinanzOnline credentials are set. For more information, see
fiskaltrust documentation
.
Fiscal audits
Fiscal audits are not pre-announced and can be conducted by the tax authorities at any given time at the store.
During an audit, auditors may check:
Legal documents related to fiscal regulations in the store.
Fiscal exports
For more information about the fiscal reports in Austria, see
Fiscal reports
.
