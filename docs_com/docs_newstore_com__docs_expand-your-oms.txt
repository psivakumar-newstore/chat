NewStore enables seamless integration with an external Order Management System (OMS) to streamline order handling, enhance order visibility, and support advanced e-commerce functions. NewStore also supports pre-orders for retailers who need them. Retailers can also use third-party e-commerce systems such as Salesforce Commerce Cloud (SFCC) to enhance catalog and inventory management for your business.
Enhanced order flexibility
Manage diverse order types, including pre-orders, to allow customers access to exclusive deals or niche experiences.
Manage pre-orders by linking them to the OMS, allowing customers to place orders for upcoming items with accurate timelines. Help customers reserve products in advance, with accurate stock and shipping estimates.
Read more
E-commerce platform support
Integrate with platforms such as SFCC for improved inventory, order, and fulfillment management.
Extend NewStore’s OMS capabilities to your e-commerce platform for efficient order flow and fulfillment.
Streamline order processing across online and physical channels, providing a unified experience for customers.

Why is it important for your business or stores?
Increases efficiency
: Automates data exchange between different order management systems, minimizing manual entry and reducing the risk of errors.
Supports scalability
: Provides flexibility to adapt to growing order volumes and complex order types as your business expands.
Enhances customer satisfaction
: Gives customers a seamless shopping experience with accurate order tracking, pre-orders, and reliable fulfillment.
