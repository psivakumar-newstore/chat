Logging into NewStore Omnichannel Manager

There are 2 ways to log into the app:
Using your corporate credentials
Using your NewStore credentials
Prerequisites
A user account that has corporate or NewStore credentials (email ID and password) for logging into NewStore Omnichannel Manager. See
Creating a user account
.
Important
User accounts can only be created by a user with the
Manage Users and Roles
permission.  see
Default roles and permissions
.
Using your corporate credentials
If
SSO
is enabled for your business, there are two ways to log into NewStore Omnichannel Manager:
With your corporate credentials
: If you already have corporate credentials assigned to you by your business.
With your credentials
from NewStore: If you do not have existing corporate credentials, or your business needs you to use NewStore credentials to log into NewStore Omnichannel Manager.
For more information on enabling SSO for your business, see
Configuring identity and access management
.
Logging in with your corporate credentials
Ensure that you know your corporate login and password.
On the login screen, click
Login with my company email
.
To log in, enter your corporate credentials in the login screen you are redirected to.
You are logged into NewStore Omnichannel Manager.
Logging in with your NewStore credentials
Ensure that you know your NewStore login and password.
On the login screen, click
or use Username and Password
.
To log in, enter your NewStore credentials in the login screen you are redirected to.
You are logged into NewStore Omnichannel Manager.
Setting up a password
To set up a password for your NewStore credentials, ensure that the password:
Contains special characters (!@#$%^&*)
Contains lower case (a-z) and upper case (A-Z) characters, and numbers (0-9)
Must be at least 8 characters in length
Must be a non-empty string
Using your NewStore credentials
If
SSO
is not enabled for your business, use your NewStore credentials to log into NewStore Omnichannel Manager. Contact the support team for more information.
Technical configuration
The following section involves working with technical configurations and APIs. Contact your IT support team for more information.
See
Configuring identity and access management
.
Ensure that you know your NewStore login and password.
On the login screen, click
Login with username and password
.
To log in, enter your NewStore credentials in the login screen you are redirected to.
You are logged into NewStore Omnichannel Manager.
